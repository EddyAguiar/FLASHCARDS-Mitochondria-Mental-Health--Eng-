🧬 What are mitochondria beyond “powerhouses”?

✅ Answer: Mitochondria regulate neurotransmitters, hormones, and inflammation—not just produce energy.

✅ Explanation: These functions are vital for cell survival and communication in the brain.

🧪 Example: Dysfunctional mitochondria may impair serotonin signaling, triggering mood issues.

🧠 What is the Brain Energy Theory?

✅ Answer: All mental disorders are metabolic disorders of the brain rooted in mitochondrial dysfunction.

✅ Explanation: Impaired brain cell energy leads to disordered thinking, mood, and behavior.

🔗 How do mitochondria affect mental health?

✅ Answer: Faulty mitochondria disrupt brain cell energy, leading to psychiatric symptoms.

✅ Explanation: Energy-starved neurons misfire, contributing to depression, anxiety, or schizophrenia.

🧪 Example: Bipolar disorder often shows mitochondrial irregularities in patient scans.

🤝 Are mental and physical disorders connected?

✅ Answer: Yes—both often share underlying mitochondrial dysfunctions.

✅ Explanation: Conditions like diabetes and depression can co-occur due to shared metabolic roots.

🏃‍♀️ How do lifestyle factors affect mitochondria?

✅ Answer: Sleep, exercise, diet, and stress directly influence mitochondrial function.

✅ Explanation: Healthy habits support energy production and reduce inflammation in the brain.

🧪 Example: Ketogenic diets may stabilize mood via improved mitochondrial function.

💊 What about medication and therapy?

✅ Answer: Many psychiatric meds improve mitochondrial and metabolic functions.

✅ Explanation: Their effectiveness may stem from enhanced cell energy and reduced stress.

🥗 Can diet help treat mental illness?

✅ Answer: Certain diets can optimize mitochondrial function and reduce symptoms.

✅ Explanation: Nutritional interventions affect brain metabolism and neuroplasticity.

😰 How does trauma impact mitochondria?

⚠️ Answer: Chronic stress increases inflammation and damages mitochondria.

⚠️ Explanation: This worsens mood disorders and cognitive decline over time.

🚀 Why is this theory revolutionary?

✅ Answer: It unifies mental and physical disorders under a metabolic lens.

✅ Explanation: It offers a science-based root cause model with lifestyle-based solutions.

🔄 What does healing look like?

✅ Answer: Supporting mitochondrial health can reduce or even reverse mental illness.

✅ Explanation: Treatments include sleep, diet, exercise, stress control, and metabolic therapies.
